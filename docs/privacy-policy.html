Splivy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Splivy ("we", "our", or "the app") helps you split and settle shared expenses with friends, family, and groups. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Splivy, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to run the app and provide its features:

  • Account information: your email address and display name, which you provide when you sign up.
  • Expense data: the expenses, groups, splits, payments, and related notes you create and share within the app.
  • Limited technical data: basic information required for authentication and to keep your data in sync across sessions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve Splivy, including to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Record, calculate, and display shared expenses and settlements.
  • Sync your data securely across your devices and group members.
  • Maintain the security, reliability, and performance of the app.

3. Data Storage and Backend

Splivy uses Supabase as its backend service to securely store and manage your account and expense data. Your data is stored on Supabase's managed infrastructure and is protected by access controls so that you and your group members can only access the data you are authorized to see. You can learn more about Supabase's own practices at supabase.com/privacy.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not use your expense data for advertising. Your data is used only to provide the Splivy service to you and your groups.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your information. You may:

  • Access and update your account details and expense data within the app.
  • Delete your expenses, groups, or your entire account at any time.
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

When you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our active systems. Some information may remain in backups for a limited period before being permanently erased.

6. Children's Privacy

Splivy is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:
